About

I was born in 1986 in Bratislava, Slovakia where I currently reside. After studying composition  at Conservatory and  Academy of Performing Arts in Bratislava, I took additional education at Universität fur Musik und darstellende Kunst in Vienna and on composition courses in Czech Republic and Austria.

As a classically trained composer  with compositional experience in numerous genres and styles, my pieces are mostly for various medias such as film, dance or theatre. In my own projects I prefer electronic / electroacoustic approach often combined with acoustic instruments.

In my classical music projects I have collaborated with number of Slovak performers, conductors, ensembles and institutions. Many of my works were performed or premierred at concerts and festivals in Slovakia (Melos Éthos, Nová slovenská hudba, NEXT etc) as well as in countries abroad (Austria, Hungary, Spain, Israel, Turkey etc).

Currently I teach art courses at Conservatory in Bratislava and co-run an association Hurhaj focused on organizing educational or concert events and creating platforms to support composers’ activities.

I invite you to explore my work. In case you are interested in collaboration, feel free to contact me.
